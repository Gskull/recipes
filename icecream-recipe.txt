Ingredients:
 - 1/2 heaped cup pistachios
 - 1 cup full fat milk
 - 2 cups whipping cream
 - 3/4 cup organic sugar
 - 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
 
Directions:
  1. Chill milk and cream. Freeze the icecream maker bowl as per the manufacturers
     instructions.
  2. Chop pistachios finely and set aside some for garnishing.
  3. Whisk together milk, vanilla and sugar to melt the sugar completely.
  4. Add the cream and whisk just to mix well.
  5. Start machine and our the mixture. Churn for 15 to 20 mins.
  6. Add the chopped nuts and mix properly.
  7. Transfer to a freezer safe bowl. Cover and freeze until set or for 6 to 8 hours.
  8. Scoop and serve pista icecream. Garnish with your favourite toppings.
